About Us

INTRODUCTION.

The Kanchanjunga Higher Secondary School is truly an institution committed towards excellence in the field of education. Its surplus extracurricular activities, games and participation in inter college events bring forth all the talents of the young minds to take the challenges of this century to stand as the best in every aspect of life.

VISION AND MISSION

A literate society with a purpose, a progressive society with ideas and a conscious society working towards establishing a better and brighter future for all.

OBJECTIVE!

The Kanchanjunga Higher Secondary School aims to become a center of learning for young students who are determined to meet the challenges posed by the increasing pace of the scientific advancements and mobilization. The Kanchanjunga Higher Secondary School not only knowing what and how but making students dents self-confident, self-disciplined and self-reliant by stressing value education, social work, counseling, career guidance, leadership training and other extracurricular activities has been the major focus at Kanchanjunga Higher Secondary School

INFRASTRUCTARE AND ENVIRONMENT

The Kanchanjunga Higher Secondary School's complex is well equipped for imparting quality education and overall growth of the students. The complexes are well furnished and equipped with modern services to facilitate teaching-learning process. We focus on providing the best possible educational environment to our students

ELIGBILITY CRITERIA AND ADMISIONS PROCEDURE

All students who have passed SEE or equivalent with minimum C grade in aggregate are eligible for the program.

EXTRA ACTIVITIES

A wide range of in house and outdoor extra-curricular activities are conducted by the school as demanded by education system and curriculum and as per interest of the students and parents. Outdoor activities include games like badminton, pool, table tennis, basketball and many more.

Sports

Sports bring students together and create a sense of community. They develop students' team spirit. It is the fastest and easiest way to make friends. Apart from the academic excellence, we encourage our students to participate in various sports organized by the college every year. We also provide ample opportunities for them to participate in various inter-college tournaments.

Project work

Project work supports students in independent learning. It naturally prepares them for argument, whether that's about the interpretation of data, the validity of a philosophical viewpoint, or something that is concrete as the design. Students seek various alternatives to think critically and evaluate the merits of different possibilities. We provide project works to our students to strengthen their learning and sometimes they are sent in the field to accomplish their assigned task.

Cultural Program

College organizes a Special Event annually with parents/ guardians and guests from various social spheres of life. Along with students presenting varieties of cultural programs, the annual progress of the college and students is monitored and shared among all.

Field teip and education visit

Excursions and Educational field trips for students are organized with prior consent from parents and guardians to enhance the knowledge of students thereby aiming at not only the classroom approach but also through practical hands-on approach. Parents are also encouraged to join on such trips
